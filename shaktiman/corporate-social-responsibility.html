CSR initiatives

Rural Infrastructure Development

  • We have financed the building of an underground sewer system with water tanks and an RCC road fitting the approach road with blocks and streetlights in the neighborhood.
  • We have renovated a heritage police station built over 100 years ago and provided for the accommodation of 35 police officers.

Health Care

  • We regularly organize Blood Donation Camps at our corporate Head Office. Most of our employees were excited to participate in the initiative by donating blood spontaneously for the well-being of the surrounding communities.
  • We donated XXXX nutrition kits for tuberculosis patients in nearby hospitals.

Environment

  • We planted over 1,000 trees at various locations.
  • We created a water reservoir at the external area of our production area Unit II.

Education

  • We funded the development of a Primary School for children in nearby areas.
  • We supported AEKRANG by renovating a building for disabled children.
  • We organized a fire safety training in a village school to raise awareness on combating fire accidents at schools. Our fire safety team conducted the session and explained the causes of fire breakouts and how to extinguish it, sensitizing on techniques and measures to prevent fire accidents. The team also performed live-demonstration on fire drills, the handling of fire extinguisher and other measures.
