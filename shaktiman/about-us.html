About us and our goals

Tirth Agro Technology Private Limited, known as Shaktiman, was founded in 1997 in Rajkot, Gujarat, by the late Shri Ladhubhai S. Gohil. Originally focused on spare parts as A.G. Agro Industries, we evolved to become a prominent Indian agricultural implements manufacturer under the brand “SHAKTIMAN.”

Our core vision is to fully mechanize Indian agriculture with a robust dealership network, world-class manufacturing, and a diverse product range. Notable achievements include being the world’s largest Rotary Tiller manufacturer and the leading exporter of agricultural implements to over 100 countries. We emphasize sustainable growth and are dedicated to research and development.

Shaktiman’s core vision

Shaktiman’s core vision is to mechanize Indian agriculture entirely, and it has made significant strides in fulfilling this vision. Over the years, the company has built a robust dealership network, with over 850 dealers, 64 distributors, and 105 parts distributors across India. Thanks to its pursuit of excellence in all aspects of the business, Shaktiman has garnered a substantial presence both in the domestic and international markets, capturing more than 50% of the domestic market share.

Shaktiman: A Leader in
Agricultural Machinery

Thanks to its pursuit of excellence in all aspects of the business, Shaktiman has garnered a substantial presence both in the domestic and international markets, capturing more than 50% of the domestic market share. The company boasts a world-class manufacturing plant covering 75 acres of land, equipped with advanced machinery capable of handling bulk orders with precision. It prides itself on using high-quality raw materials and sustainable engineering designs to produce innovative implements that meet international standards.

Shaktiman is renowned for its leadership in various agricultural equipment, including Rotary Tillers, Mulchers, Balers, Mobile Shredders, Power Harrows, Self-Propelled Boom Sprayers, and Sugar Cane Harvesters. The brand has gained a reputation as a trusted provider of best-in-class products and services, covering everything from seed bed preparation to crop residue management.

Shaktiman: Enhancing Economic Efficiency in Agriculture

Global Leadership

Shaktiman is the world’s largest manufacturer of Rotary Tillers and the top-selling brand of Rotavators. It is also the number one exporter of agricultural implements to over 100 countries

Сontribution to Agriculture

Shaktiman plays a significant role in advancing Indian agriculture, making farming more economical and efficient through world-class implements and services.

Diverse Product Portfolio

Shaktiman offers a wide range of products, covering all aspects of the crop cycle, including advanced machinery like the Big Sugarcane Harvester, Pneumatic Planters, and more.
