SMS & Push Notifications

When timing matters, speed and visibility become critical. SMS and push notifications deliver messages directly to the devices investors check first-their phones-ensuring important updates are seen immediately.

At LFG Equities, SMS and push notifications are used selectively and strategically to complement longer-form communication channels, not replace them.

What Are
SMS Alerts?

SMS alerts are direct text messages sent straight to a user’s phone, delivering updates instantly and with virtually guaranteed visibility. Unlike emails or in-app notifications, SMS messages appear directly on a user’s lock screen—no apps to open, no feeds to scroll through—which is why SMS boasts ~99% delivery rates and extremely high visibility. Whenever you receive a text, you see it immediately, making SMS one of the most reliable ways to ensure critical updates are noticed.

Our SMS Distribution Network

While newsletters provide long-form, content-rich updates, SMS focuses on speed, visibility, and direct impact. Newsletters can land in crowded inboxes and may require readers to open an email or app before viewing the content. SMS, on the other hand, cuts through all friction, delivering a short, high-priority message right to the device people check first—their phone’s lock screen.

This makes SMS ideal for time-sensitive announcements, breaking news, urgent updates, or reminders that need immediate attention.

How SMS Differs from Newsletters

Most of our newsletter publishers also operate parallel SMS lists, giving us access to audiences across both email and text. Because SMS is more personal, more noticeable, and nearly impossible to miss, these lists often carry significantly higher value compared to traditional email-only distributions. For many investors and subscribers, SMS alerts function as their first—and most trusted—source of timely market updates and company news.

Push Notifications

What Are Push Notifications?

Push notifications on messaging apps work similarly to SMS—messages are delivered instantly and can appear directly on a user’s lock screen. The key difference is platform preference: some users opt to receive alerts through their favorite messaging apps (such as WhatsApp, Telegram) instead of traditional SMS.

Why They Matter

Messaging app push notifications are a powerful way to connect with audiences who rely on these apps as part of their daily communication. They provide:

Instant delivery with lock-screen visibility

High engagement from users accustomed to chat-style interactions

A familiar format that feels personal and conversational

Global reach without the costs or restrictions of SMS

How We Use Them

Our system leverages push notifications across various messaging platforms, allowing subscribers to receive timely updates on their preferred channel. This ensures that important alerts reach users quickly, boosting engagement and complementing email and SMS campaigns for maximum impact.
