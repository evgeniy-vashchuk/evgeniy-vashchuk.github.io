Premium livestock, pet food, wildlife, and animal feed & nutrition available through trusted local feed dealers across the United States.
Manufactured in Georgia. Trusted nationwide since 1991.

Mid-South Feeds has built a nationwide network of trusted dealers who share our commitment to quality animal nutrition. Our dealer partners are local experts who understand the unique needs of livestock owners, pet enthusiasts, and wildlife managers in their communities. When you purchase Mid-South Feeds products through an authorized dealer, you're getting more than premium feed—you're gaining access to knowledgeable support and personalized service.

Our comprehensive product line includes specialized formulas for cattle, horses, goats, sheep, poultry, swine, pets, and wildlife. Each formula is manufactured at our Georgia facility using carefully selected ingredients and rigorous quality control processes.
