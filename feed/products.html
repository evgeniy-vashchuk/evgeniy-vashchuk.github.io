Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the product.

Our feed solutions are not one-size-fits-all. Whether you’re feeding beef cattle, dairy cows, or specialty breeds, we have a feed formulation that matches your specific needs. Our cattle livestock feed options are created to improve:

  • Weight gain and muscle development
  • Milk production and quality
  • Overall health and disease resistance

Our commitment to using organic trace minerals sets us apart from the competition. Unlike conventional inorganic minerals, organic trace minerals are chelated or bound to organic molecules, making them significantly more bioavailable and easier for animals to absorb.
