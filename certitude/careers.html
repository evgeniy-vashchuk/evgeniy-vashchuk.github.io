Why Certitude?

At Certitude, we recognize that our success begins and ends with the quality of our investigators. That’s why we treat our team—whether full-time employees or contract collaborators—as our greatest asset.

We operate a flexible employment model that enables experienced investigators to do meaningful work, supported by a positive, objective-focused team. Our investigators enjoy the benefits of independence with access to administrative support, peer reviews, and client management.

We value professionalism, neutrality, and diverse lived experiences—and we’re committed to supporting your best work.
