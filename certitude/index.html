Professional Workplace Investigators

The Certitude Group is a workplace investigation firm specializing in lawyer-led, legally defensible workplace investigations. Based in Atlantic Canada, our roster of investigators have the specialized skills and experience required to navigate the most sensitive and complex cases of harassment and misconduct in the workplace.

With services offered in both official languages, an active roster of lawyer-investigators with deep experience across industries and regulatory environments, Certitude Group is the one call you need to make when you need a workplace investigation done right.

About Us

The Certitude Group is made up of experienced professional workplace investigators serving Atlantic Canada and beyond in both official languages. Because the majority our investigators are trained lawyers, we approach each investigation with a clear process and defensible evidentiary standards. This gives our clients peace of mind in knowing that the decisions they make based on our investigations, will be legally defensible should that need arise.

No organization is happy to have to engage an external workplace investigator, but the right investigator can discretely bring closure to difficult situations. With each investigation we conduct, our goal remains the same, to allow all parties to move forward with clear information.
