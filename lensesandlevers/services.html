Explore 10 Types of Innovation

Most breakthrough innovations are comprised of some combination of the following 10 types. At the beginning of each innovation cycle, we work with client-partners to determine which types of innovation will be the focus of our efforts. In some cases, the cycle mandate is intentionally broad, giving teams room to explore. In other cases, the cycle mandate is narrow, with a focus on only one or two types of innovation.
