Our people

A Growing Network of
Innovation Collaborators
and Advisors

One of the truly unique elements of our service innovation process, is that we assemble innovation teams made up of client-partner members and individually selected collaborators and advisors from industry. We look for collaborators with T-shaped skills, that is a depth of expertise in a single field and a proven ability to collaborate across disciplines. There are 4 primary roles we look to fill on nearly every innovation team we assemble, which are:

  • Entrepreneur

  • Designer

  • Engineer

  • Accountant

Complementary roles are then added to achieve the right balance of perspectives, best suited to the innovation challenge. This unique approach to collaborative innovation is our “secret sauce”.

Matthew MacQuarrie

Founder, Cycle Lead

Matthew has worked in numerous industries, domestically and internationally, including finance and banking, metals and mining, advertising and media, food and beverage, government, ecommerce, and information technology.

He has held senior executive roles in marketing, sales, and strategic growth and innovation. He is also a successful entrepreneur, having founded two companies that grew to employ dozens of employees and generate 8-figure revenues.

I believe that creativity is the fuel that powers innovation. But creativity alone is not enough, it has to be filtered by expertise and experience. To do this well, a good process is key. I have long been fascinated with the process of harnessing creativity to solve problems and create value.

Creativity is the world’s most valuable resource and the one we are most willing to share. It’s a beautiful paradox.
