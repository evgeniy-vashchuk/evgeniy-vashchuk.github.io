Become an
Innovation Collaborator

Are you an experienced professional with specialized skills? Do you enjoy collaborating with individuals who have skills, experiences, and perspectives that are different than your own? If so, we’d like to hear from you.

We assemble innovation teams made up of client-partner members and individually selected collaborators from industry. There are 4 primary roles we look to fill on nearly every innovation team, they are:

  • The Entrepreneur

    The professional risk taker who understands how to calculate the odds. A bold leader who uses well-honed creative problem-solving skills to create value.

  • The Designer

    The insight hunter with the ability to translate human needs into elegant products and services. Naturally empathetic and a champion of solutions that address user needs in original ways.

  • The Engineer

    The seeker of quantifiable truths who understands and navigates within the constraints of technology. A natural builder who understands what’s required to move from idea to reality.

  • The Accountant

    The practical leader with a mind for numbers and a steadfast focus on the bottom-line. A champion of solutions that are rooted in financial viability.

What Does an Innovation Collaborator Do?

You will bring an external perspective and complementary skills to an Internal Innovation Team during an innovation cycle. In practical terms, this means spending 1-2 hours per week thinking about and discussing an innovation challenge as member of the team for a period of approximately 70 days.

Why Become an Innovation Collaborator?

There are two reasons why someone chooses to share their insights and experience as a member of an innovation team: to grow professionally and to get paid.

Get Paid

Earn Commission

We are always on the lookout for interesting innovation challenges. As such, we offer a commission for referrals that turn into engagements. Commission amounts can be substantial depending upon the nature of the engagement. Some conditions apply.

An Honorarium

As an Innovation Collaborator, you will receive an honorarium upon the completion of the innovation cycle. This amount will vary depending on the client-partner and the focus of the cycle.

Professional Growth

Develop Your Skills

When you participate in a Lenses and Levers Innovation Cycle, you are part of a multi-disciplinary team of highly skilled professionals searching for game changing innovations, within a condensed schedule. You’ll plug into a proven process utilized by some of the world’s most innovative brands. Needless to say, you’ll need to bring your A-game.

Scratch Your Creative Itch

Step outside of your comfort zone and let your creativity run wild. As a collaborator you’ll be asked to leverage your creative problem-solving skills to contribute to a broad array of ideas from the safe and boring, to the whacky and game-changing.

Find New Opportunities

We take care to match the right collaborators with the right client-partner organizations. We focus on fit, and as a result many collaborators have continued to work with client-partners after their initial cycle has been completed. An innovation cycle is a great way to demonstrate your value to an organization.
