Our Cycles

We help organizations solve problems and create value by engaging and empathizing with the people they serve. Our collaborative human-centered innovation cycles use multi-disciplinary teams of client and non-client collaborators, sponsor user panels, and subject matter experts to systematically build what’s next.

Desirable, Feasible, Viable

Our unique process is designed to explore solutions through the lenses of desirability, feasibility, & viability. Starting with a focus on the people being served, we are able to generate solutions are desirable to users. This however is only one lens, and we then narrow our focus to understand which solutions are technically feasible & financially viable. Reimagining solutions from multiple perspectives requires cross-functional collaboration. We bring together the perspectives that matter and bridge the gap between inspiration & execution.
